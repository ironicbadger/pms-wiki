## Notes

This episode ranks Jellyfin clients for Apple TV across setup, library browsing, playback, format support, player controls, extra features, and overall polish.

The tvOS Jellyfin client ecosystem is changing quickly, so treat the ranking as a snapshot from June 2026 rather than a permanent verdict.
